Problem Statement

📍 The Challenge

The traditional Indian men’s wear industry is a ₹40,000+ Crore market, yet specialized boutiques like Scandal (Thane) remain restricted to local foot traffic due to a lack of digital infrastructure. Without an online presence, customers are forced to visit physically, while the absence of size guidance and secure remote payment options leads to high return rates and lost growth potential. To address this, there is a clear need for a full-stack, responsive e-commerce platform that digitizes the shopping experience offering personalized AI-driven recommendations and secure checkouts to help local heritage brands scale sustainably in the modern economy.

  • No remote ordering or catalog browsing
  • High return rates due to sizing uncertainty
  • Limited reach beyond local geography

SDG 12: Promoting responsible production by reducing sizing returns and supporting local heritage craftsmanship.

Project Objectives

  • Premium UI/UX: Design a high-fidelity, responsive frontend using Vanilla JS and CSS3 with glassmorphism.
  • Robust Backend: Implement a scalable Node.js & Express server with secure authentication.
  • PostgreSQL Database: Migrate to a relational architecture for superior data integrity and ACID compliance.
  • AI Assistance: Build an AI Wardrobe Stylist and a Size Consultant to enhance the user shopping experience.
